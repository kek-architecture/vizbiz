About our event

The Hungarian Contemporary Architecture Centre - KÉK and Brick Visual present a full day conference dedicated to the business benefits of architecture visualization.

In order to stay competitive, architecture practices need to adapt quickly to the constantly changing professional environment. Architecture visualization (ArchViz) tools with the plethora of new technological solutions have the potential of positively influence and even catalyze this adaptation process. By using and developing the latest technologies, such as augmented and virtual reality, architects have the freedom to showcase their work in a highly engaging way, like never before. Visual content is essential to translate ideas and convince clients, competition juries and developers. There are several examples of practices who by investing in ArchViz, can leverage its commercial benefits from increased customer satisfaction, sales performance and revenue. Whether building up in-house 3D teams, or outsourcing to ArchViz firms, gaining skills and expertise in ArchViz can make a difference in your architecture practice.

The Hungarian Contemporary Architecture Centre - KÉK teamed up with Brick Visual, a high-end architectural visualization company to organise a full day conference dedicated to “Viz Business”. Brick Visual’s industry know-how and international connections have inspired a stunning program with great speakers at the forefront of their industry. They will tell directly about their experiences, focusing on what works and what does not, sharing what tools and processes have had a beneficial impact on their practices. The conference provides first-hand insights into how to maximise production effectiveness, optimize workflow to help you to learn how to engage with ArchViz.

The workshop on the second day will be a practice-based, inspiring demo-day at Brick Visual’s Office, completed with a visualization class, designed for architects.

The accompanying exhibition, The Art of Viz Business is showcasing world-class ArchViz studios based in Budapest delivering award winning designs to top architecture firms globally.
