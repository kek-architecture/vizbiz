About our event

The Hungarian Contemporary Architecture Centre - KÉK and Brick Visual presents a full day conference dedicated to the business benefits of architecture visualization.

In order to stay competitive architecture practices have to learn how to adapt quickly to the constantly changing professional environment. Architecture visualization (AV) tools with the plethora of new technological solutions have the potential of positively influence this adaptation process. There are several examples of practices who by investing in AV, can leverage its commercial benefits from increased customer satisfaction, sales performance and revenue (ide be kéne rakni, hogy mik a business benefitek pontosan). Whether building up in-house 3D teams, or outsourcing to AV firms, gaining skills and expertise in AV can make a difference in your architecture practice.

The Hungarian Contemporary Architecture Centre - KÉK has partnered with Brick Visual to organise a full day conference dedicated to “Viz Business”. KÉK, an independent professional organization committed to the dissemination of architecture practice, has long-term expertise in setting up international events. Brick Visual is a high-end architectural visualization company, who creates engaging visual content for architectural studios as….BIG, Snohetta or Zaha Hadid Studio. Brick Visual’s industry know-how and international connections have inspired a stunning program with great speakers at the forefront of their industry. They will tell directly about their experiences, focusing on what works and what does not, sharing what tools and processes have had a beneficial impact on their practices. The conference provides first-hand insights into how to maximise production effectiveness, optimize workflow to help you to learn how to engage with ArchViz. Join a full day conference where speakers at the forefront of the industry help you identify the right tools and processes.

The workshop on the second day will be a practice-based, inspiring demo-day in the Brick Visual Office, completed with a visualization class, designed for architects.

The accompanying exhibition showcasing world-class AV studios based in Budapest delivering award winning designs to top architecture firms globally.
